About Irene Phoebe Kiwia

Irene Phoebe Kiwia is an author and podcast host focused on sharing ideas, conversations, and digital work that inform, challenge, and inspire. Her work spans writing, audio conversations, and creative content, with an emphasis on clarity, growth, and meaningful engagement.

Irene’s work is rooted in thoughtful expression and honest conversation. Through books, podcasts, and digital content, she creates space for ideas, stories, and discussions that resonate across different experiences and perspectives.

Her platform is designed to remain open and evolving — allowing her to explore diverse topics, share insights, and connect with audiences without limitation. Each project reflects intention, depth, and a commitment to quality.

This website serves as a central hub for Irene’s published works, audio content, and future creative projects.
